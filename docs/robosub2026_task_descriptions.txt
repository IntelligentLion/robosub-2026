ROBOSUB 2026 - SECTION 3.2: TASK DESCRIPTIONS
===============================================

THEME: "Restore and Recovery"
A storm swept through, leaving damaged infrastructure, disrupted communication,
and environmental hazards. Two roles:
- SURVEY & REPAIR: Damage assessment, inspect area, repair infrastructure
- SEARCH & RESCUE: Search debris, restore safety and functionality

No three elements (launch point, gate, tasks) are along a line.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.1 HEADING OUT (COIN FLIP)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Teams may orient AUV in any direction from designated starting location
- All vehicles must BEGIN ON SURFACE, SUBMERGE BEFORE LEAVING START ZONE
- Optional coin flip for extra points:
  * HEADS: AUV positioned approximately parallel to gate
  * TAILS: AUV positioned with tail approximately facing gate (backward)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.2 TASK 1 - BEGIN ASSESSMENT (GATE)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Gate construction:
- PVC pipe floating just below surface, moored to bottom
- 3-inch wide panels: BLACK and RED corrugated plastic
- RIGHT side: RED upper half, BLACK lower half (Red, Right, Above)
- LEFT side: BLACK upper half, RED lower half
- Gate dimensions: 120 in x 60 in (3m x 1.5m)

Gate divider:
- 2-inch RED corrugated flat sheet divides gate in half
- One side has compass/hammer_pick image (SURVEY & REPAIR)
- Other side has ring_buoy/SOS image (SEARCH & RESCUE)
- AUV chooses role by passing under a specific side

Role icons:
- Survey & Repair: compass / hammer_pick
- Search & Rescue: ring_buoy / SOS

Style points:
- Every 90-degree change in orientation = accumulated points
- Returning to previous orientation doesn't count
- Roll and Pitch worth MORE than Yaw

Capability Matrix:
- Core: Pass through the gate
- Advanced: Coin flip determines orientation
- Disruptive: Random role assignment (coin flip or visual indicator)

Scoring:
- Pass through gate
- Maintain control
- Coin flip (heading)
- Coin flip (role)
- Style: Yaw, Roll/Pitch

If visual indicator not available, coin flip assigns role.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.3 TASK 2 - AVOID DEBRIS (SLALOM)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Sets of vertical RED and WHITE PVC pipes (1 inch, 3 ft / 0.9m tall)
- Moored at different heights to floor, floating vertically
- Arrangement: WHITE left, RED middle, WHITE right
- THREE sets of pipes, points for each navigated

More points for:
- Staying on SAME SIDE of red pipe as gate passage
  (if red gate divider was on right, pass with red pipe on right)
- Staying WITHIN the area of the pipes (not above, not below)
  As long as part of AUV breaks plane of pipes = inside

Capability Matrix:
- Core: Identify and stay within slalom
- Advanced: Navigate correct side
- Disruptive: None

Scoring:
- Navigate debris: any, correct side
- Correct depth

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.4 TASK 3 - RECON (BINS)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- 3D pipeline made from PVC, supported off pool bottom
- Four bins on sides of pipeline (2 per role)
- Inside bins: fire emoji (Survey & Repair), blood emoji (Search & Rescue)
- Near each bin: a light and magnetic detector

At run start, light is ON signaling need for repair/rescue.

Points for:
- Dropping up to 2 markers into bins (based on where marker rests)
- More points for markers in bins matching AUV's role
- Additional points for interacting with magnetic detector on pipeline
- Additional points for turning light off

Note: Light specifications still being determined.

Capability Matrix:
- Core: Drop markers in bin
- Advanced: Drop markers in correct bin
- Disruptive: (tentative) Interact with light

Scoring:
- Drop marker(s) in: any, correct bin
- (tentative) Interact with light

Bin dimensions: 24 in x 12 in x 6 in (610mm x 305mm x 152mm)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.5 TASK 4 - DEPLOY (TORPEDOES)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- One vertical board with images on front side
- Survey & Repair: fire / fire_engine
- Search & Rescue: blood / ambulance
- Two different board versions, two different size openings

Points for:
- Firing torpedoes through ANY opening (must pass through for full points)
- Partial points: torpedo touches board without passing through
- Maximum points: fire through LARGER opening first, then SMALLER opening
  * Survey: fire (large) then fire_engine (small)
  * Rescue: blood (large) then ambulance (small)
- Distance bonus: far (1 ft) and farther (1.5 ft)

Capability Matrix:
- Core: Fire torpedo through any opening
- Advanced: Fire torpedoes in correct sequence (large, small)
- Disruptive: Fire torpedoes in correct sequence (large, small)

Board dimensions: 2 ft x 2 ft (0.6m x 0.6m)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.6 TASK 5 - RESUPPLY (OCTAGON)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- 9 ft (2.7m) diameter octagon floating on surface
- Acoustic pinger on floor near center guides AUV
- Hanging from octagon (facing inward): compass, hammer_pick, ring_buoy, SOS images
- Center: Resupply table (2ft x 2ft)
- On table, two items per role:
  * Survey & Repair: nut_and_bolt + electric_plug
  * Search & Rescue: pill + adhesive_bandage
- Two opposite edges: baskets for items
  * Survey basket bottom: warning sign
  * Rescue basket bottom: helmet_with_cross

Points for:
- Surfacing inside octagon
- Surfacing with each item (stay inside to continue)
- Facing any image hanging from octagon (max for correct based on role+items):
  * 1 item in basket: face compass (Survey) or ring_buoy (Rescue)
  * 2+ items in basket: face hammer_pick (Survey) or SOS (Rescue)
- Moving items to any basket (max for correct role basket)
- Rotation bonus: rotate same number of turns as items in baskets
  (3 items = rotate 3 times)

Capability Matrix:
- Core: Surface in octagon
- Advanced: Pick up and deliver at least one object to any basket
- Disruptive: Face correct image

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.7 TASK 6 - RETURN HOME
------------------------------------------------------------------------
At end of run, WHILE UNDERWATER, AUV passes back through start gate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.8 PATH
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Path markers: ~4 ft (~1.2m) long x 6 in (15cm) wide
- Color: ORANGE
- Straight segments, points to next task
- No points awarded for following path (guidance only)

ONLY TWO PATH MARKERS:
1. At gate -> points to Avoid Debris (Slalom)
2. From Avoid Debris -> points to Recon (Bins)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2.9 PINGER
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Located very close to task (next to or center)
- No points for finding pinger (guidance only)

TWO PINGERS:
1. At Deploy (Torpedoes)
2. At Resupply (Octagon)
