ROBOSUB 2026 - SECTION 3.1: MANDATORY ACTIVITIES
=================================================

Before entering Autonomy Challenge, teams must demonstrate:
- Vehicle requirements (section 5.3)
- Safe control ability via: static safety inspection + weight measurement

3.1.1 STATIC SAFETY INSPECTION
- All systems must pass safety inspection before water operation (section 5.2)
- Course Director may DQ unsafe vehicles

3.1.2 WEIGHT MEASUREMENT
- Vehicles weighed before each semi-finals and finals run
- Teams transport vehicle on cart to scale

WEIGHT TABLE:
- > 60 kg (132 lbs): DISQUALIFIED
- 60 kg >= weight > 38 kg (132 >= weight > 84 lbs): PENALTY = -(250 + 11*(Weight_kg - 60))
- 38 kg >= weight > 22 kg (84 >= weight > 48.5 lbs): BONUS = 4.4*(38 - Weight_kg)
- <= 22 kg (<= 48.5 lbs): BONUS = 80 + 2.2*(22 - Weight_kg)

Examples:
- 50kg: -(250+11(50-60)) = -140 penalty
- 25kg: 4.4(38-25) = 57 bonus
- 45kg + 18kg (two vehicles): -85 + 89 = 4 points
