ROBOSUB 2026 - SECTION 3.3: VEHICLE OPERATIONS
================================================

3.3.1 LAUNCH/RECOVERY
- Teams responsible for safe launch/retrieval
- Lower/raise from pool side at designated section (A, B, C, D)
- Pool: 7 ft (2.1m) deep, 1 ft (0.3m) drop from deck to water surface
- 10 ft (3m) long pipe with four-point harness provided at each section
- Two team members lift vehicle, position over water, lower in
- Reach down from edge to unhook four connection points
- Reverse procedure to retrieve

Sections A and D: recommend bridging the corner
Sections B and C: recommend someone in water to keep vehicle off wall

3.3.2 HARNESSING THE AUV
- ALL teams required to use harness/sling for launch/retrieval
- Vehicle may need to be suspended for weighing
- Integrate sling attachment points during design
- Hard points on vehicle needed (strong, secure attachment)
- Options: chain, rope, sewn slings (runners)
- Use carabiners for attachment (e.g., Black Diamond Oval)
- KISS principle: easy setup, quick attach/detach

Recommended knots:
- Figure 8 Rewoven: ~80% strength, easy to tie/inspect
- Bowline: ~70% strength, easy to untie after loading
- Double/Triple Fisherman's: best for joining ropes

3.3.3 TEAM SWIMMERS
- Teams may volunteer swimmer(s) for in-water safety/ops
- Swimmer must tread water for 1 minute each water entry
- Pool is 7 feet deep everywhere
- Can use another team's swimmer
- Semi-Finals, Third-Chance, Finals: NO team swimmers allowed
- Only during Practice/Qualifying runs
- Bring own swimming equipment (masks, snorkels, fins)
